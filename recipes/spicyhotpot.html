Spicy HotPot

Spicy HotPot Description

Spicy HotPot, otherwise known as "Malatang," is flavored with Sichuan chilli peppers and dried chilli peppers. It is the most delicious dish known to all of humanity, and if you disagree, the Hot Pot Gods will dunk your head into a bowl of boiling spicy hotpot.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Fresh and Instant Noodles
  • Quail Eggs
  • Lettuce
  • Spinach
  • Wintermelon
  • Zucchini
  • Sweet potato
  • Dried and frozen tofu
  • Beef balls
  • Fish balls
  • Dumplings
