Beef NoodleSoup

Beef NoodleSoup Description

Beef NoodleSoup is my mother's signature dish! I am always happy whenever we have beef noodle soup. It is really comforting and hearty, and I hope it is my last meal.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Fresh Noodles
  • Green Onions
  • Soy Sauce
  • Onions
  • Sesame Oil
  • Carrots
  • Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Brown the meat over the stove.
  2. Add onion and carrots, saute.
  3. Pour in soy sauce and half a liter of water.
  4. Boil.
  5. Prepare noodles.
  6. Sprinkle green onions and drizzle sesame oil over the finished bowl.
