Korean TofuSoup

Korean TofuSoup Description

Korean TofuSoup consists of extra soft tofu, thinly sliced meat, and kimchi. Occasionally, you might add a soft-cooked egg.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Fresh and Instant Noodles
  • Eggs
  • Kimchi

Steps

  1. Heat chili oil, garlic, and chili powder.
  2. Add kelp and anchovy stock.
  3. Continue boiling.
  4. Prepare and add toppings.
